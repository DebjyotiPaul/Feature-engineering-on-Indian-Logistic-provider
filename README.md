# Feature-Engineering-on-Indian-Logistics-provider
Delhivery is one of the largest and fastest-growing fully integrated player in India. The Data team at Delhivery builds intelligence and capabilities using this data that helps them to widen the gap between the quality, efficiency, and profitability of their business versus their competitor. 

## Problem Statement
The company wants to understand and process the data coming out of data engineering pipelines:
* Clean, sanitize and manipulate data to get useful features out of raw fields
* Make sense out of the raw data and help the data science team to build forecasting models on it
